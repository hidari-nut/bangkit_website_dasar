Biographies

Umi is a an athletic girl who excels at almost every sport. She has a very energetic personality to the point that she cannot stand being inactive. Umi is not only good with active sports like football or basketball; she is also good with gymnastics, and she took ballet lessons when she was younger. All of this made her body very flexible. While she is a sporty girl, she's definitely not a tomboy and she's happy to wear cute dresses.
